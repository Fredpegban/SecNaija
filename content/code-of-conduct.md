
# SecNaija Professional Code of Conduct

## 1. Purpose & Scope
This Code of Conduct outlines the ethical and professional standards expected of all SecNaija personnel, including guards, technicians, and partners. Our goal is to maintain the highest standards of safety and public trust in Nigeria.

## 2. Professional Conduct & Respect
*   Maintain a disciplined, professional appearance.
*   Treat all community members, residents, and clients with dignity and respect.
*   No discrimination based on gender, ethnicity, religion, or social status.

## 3. Lawful Operations
*   **No Vigilantism:** Personnel must never engage in unauthorized law enforcement or "jungle justice."
*   **Weapon Prohibition:** Unauthorized weapons (firearms, etc.) are strictly prohibited unless explicitly authorized by the state and the organization for specific high-risk escort duties.
*   **Authority Cooperation:** Always cooperate with the Nigerian Police Force (NPF) and NSCDC.

## 4. Use of Force
*   De-escalation and verbal communication must always be the first line of defense.
*   Physical force is a last resort and must be proportional, lawful, and documented.

## 5. Confidentiality & Privacy
*   CCTV footage and client site data are strictly confidential.
*   Do not share incident details or residential gate info on social media.

## 6. Prohibited Actions
*   **Zero Tolerance for Bribery:** Do not solicit or accept bribes (settlements) from anyone.
*   **No Extortion:** Do not use your position to intimidate or extort members of the public.
*   **Theft & Collusion:** Immediate termination for collusion with criminals or involvement in theft.

## 7. Incident Reporting
*   All incidents must be documented digitally via the SecNaija portal.
*   Provide honest, factual accounts without exaggeration or omission.

## 8. Acknowledgement
By signing this document or submitting an application to SecNaija, I acknowledge that I have read, understood, and will strictly adhere to these standards.
